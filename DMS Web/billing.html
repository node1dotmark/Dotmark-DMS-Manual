Billing

  1. Billing collects all the charges records.
  2. Fees in the left navigation bar includes all the transactions.
  3. It is sub divided into :Payment, Invoice, Refund,Checkout and Billing.
  4. Payment is only used for the IPD patients and is unavailable for other patients.
  5. Checkout keeps the records of all the unpaid transactions.
  6. It consists of the Encounter button which lists all the encounters a patient has.
  7. The uncleared encounters are in Boldface align. Once the payment is cleared, the encounter does not remain in Bold form.
  8. This bold and unbold action shows us either there is some amount to be paid or not.
  9. Once you choose the encounter and click on Pay button, it leads to the new payment window.
  10. You fill the discount(either in % or Rupees), Payment Type and the Paid Amount.
  11. Once the Payment amount is chosen, the Change amount is calculated.
  12. The notes(if necessary) is visible during print, if the content is pade public but only visible to the user if the note is private.
  13. Save Button saves the invoice whereas the Save and Print button saves and prints the invoice at the same time.
  14. You can also select encounter and add items(like Ambulance, card Reprint,etc) along with their quantity. Once added the payment window is also visible in the checkout.

Refund

  1. Once the checkout is done, the invoice is saved along with a specified Invoice number with the help of that id,refund is possible.
  2. Submit button after the invoice Id clicking leads to the refund window.
  3. You can input the valid refund quantity and the return amount and click Refund.
  4. Refund process needs an authorized username and password.
  5. The Authorized By (includes the username), Password and valid reason fields are to be filled and submit button click refunds the invoice.
  6. Refunded invoice is seen below the same window once refunded.

Invoice

  1. Invice button present above the Refund option keeps the records of all the paid invoices from the checkout.
  2. Clicking the Invoice id opens the new invoice window.
  3. You can reprint the invoice if required.
  4. The invoice table in the action field where invoice refund is done consists of Show returned Invoice
  5. Show returned Invoice shows the return details.
  6. You can hide or show the invoice details.
